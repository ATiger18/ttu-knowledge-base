Texas Tech University Knowledge Base

Quick Help for Students, Faculty, and Staff

How to Submit a FAFSA Application

  • Visit fafsa.gov and click “Start Here.”
  • Use TTU’s school code: 003644.
  • Complete and submit the form using your FSA ID.
  • Wait for confirmation through Raiderlink or TTU email.

Reserving a Group Study Room

  • Visit library.ttu.edu.
  • Log in with your eRaider.
  • Select date, time, and room.
  • Check your TTU email for confirmation.

How to Get a Parking Permit

  • Visit parking.ttu.edu.
  • Log in and choose "Purchase Permit."
  • Enter your vehicle info and pay online.

Accessing TTU DegreeWorks

  • Login to Raiderlink > "MyTech" > "DegreeWorks."
  • Review degree audit and track progress.
  • Use the "What-If" feature to explore other majors.

Declaring or Changing Your Major

  • Contact your current academic advisor.
  • Some colleges may require forms or an advising session.
  • Changes typically reflect within 3–5 business days.

Requesting a TTU Transcript

  • Unofficial: Raiderlink > "Transcript View – Unofficial."
  • Official: Visit transcripts.ttu.edu
  • Choose delivery method and follow instructions.

Downloading Microsoft Office for Free

  • Go to office.com and sign in with your TTU email.
  • Click “Install Office.”
  • Activate using your TTU credentials.

Joining a Student Organization

  • Visit ttu.campuslabs.com.
  • Browse organizations by category.
  • Click “Join” or contact group leaders to get started.

Paying Tuition and Fees

  • Access Raiderlink > “Student Business Services.”
  • Choose your payment method.
  • Check for billing deadlines and payment plans.

Using the TTU Bus System

  • Download the DoubleMap app for live tracking.
  • Buses are free with a valid TTU ID.
  • Visit citibus.com/ttu for schedules.

Setting Up a Tuition Payment Plan

  • Go to Raiderlink > “Student Business Services.”
  • Select “eBill” and then “Payment Plans.”
  • Choose a schedule and pay your first installment.

Applying for On-Campus Jobs

  • Visit ttu.joinhandshake.com.
  • Log in with your eRaider and search for jobs.
  • Upload your resume and apply directly through Handshake.

Contacting Your Academic Advisor

  • Go to Raider Success Hub > "My Success Team" to find your advisor.
  • Schedule appointments or email them directly.

Using Interlibrary Loan

  • Visit illiad.lib.ttu.edu and log in.
  • Submit requests for articles or books.
  • Track delivery via email or library account.

Resetting Your eRaider Password

  • Visit eraider.ttu.edu.
  • Select “Forgot Password” and follow prompts.
  • Answer security questions or use recovery options.

Accessing Mental Health Services

  • Student Counseling Center: (806) 742-3674
  • 24/7 Crisis Line: (806) 742-5555
  • Workshops, groups, and walk-ins are available weekly.

Applying for Graduation

  • Log in to Raiderlink > “Apply to Graduate.”
  • Select your degree and term.
  • Submit before the semester deadline.

Uploading Documents to Raider Success Hub

  • Visit success.ttu.edu.
  • Log in and go to “Documents.”
  • Upload degree plans, forms, or appeals securely.

Applying for TTU Scholarships

  • Go to ttu.academicworks.com.
  • Complete the general scholarship application.
  • Apply for additional recommended opportunities.
